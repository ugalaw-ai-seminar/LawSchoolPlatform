How to Get Your Free API Key
  1. Click the aistudio.google.com link above and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Click "Create API Key" and select the default project (or create a new one).
  3. Click "Set up billing" for the key — choose the FREE trial option (no payment required).
  4. Once your key is created and billing is active, copy the key (it starts with "AIza...").
  5. Paste it into the field above and click Enter Platform!
Still not working? Make sure billing is activated on your key — even the free trial requires this step. Also double-check that you copied the full key with no extra spaces.
